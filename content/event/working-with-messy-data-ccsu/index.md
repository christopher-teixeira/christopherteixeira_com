# Abstract
abstract: >
  Data, no matter how prepared, usually needs some refinement before it's ready to use in your analysis. During this talk, you'll hear about some approaches to exploring data, recognizing common data quality issues, and what can be done to address those issues. You'll hear about some practical approaches you can use in every project, and about some cautionary tales about what to avoid or how your decisions can start to influence the results of a project.

## Event Details

CCSU is excited to invite students, faculty, and alumni to MathXperience: Innovate, Compete, Connect, a hands-on event highlighting how mathematics connects to real careers.

Participants will:
- Hear a keynote talk on Working with Messy Data by Christopher Teixeira
- Participate in a Resume Workshop led by professionals from Travelers
- Connect with industry professionals and alumni
- Compete in a student team challenge and win prizes

This event is organized by the CCSU Department of Mathematical Sciences in partnership with the Travelers EDGE Program.
